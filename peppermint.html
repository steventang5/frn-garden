Peppermint

Benefits

  • Digestive Support: Peppermint is often used to ease indigestion and bloating.
  • Respiratory Relief: Its menthol content can help open up nasal passages during colds.
  • Culinary and Medicinal: Widely used in teas, desserts, and even some savory dishes due to its strong, refreshing flavor.

Maintenance

  • Light: Prefers partial shade(3-6 hours of sunlight) too much direct sunlight in hot climates might cause wilting.
  • Watering: Water daily during warm weather or every other day during cold weather to keep the soil moist but avoid waterlogging.

Recipes

  • Peppermint Tea: Use fresh leaves to make a soothing, aromatic tea.
  • Mint Chocolate Desserts: Infuse desserts like chocolate mousse or brownies with a hint of peppermint.
  • Smoothies: Blend fresh leaves into fruit smoothies for an extra layer of flavor.
