Basil

Benefits

  • Flavor Enchancer: Offers a robust, aromatic flavor that's perfect for Italian cuisine.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Basil contains essential oils with potential anti-inflammatory properties.
  • Nutrient Dense: Rich in vitamins A, K, and antioxidants which support overall health.

Maintenance

  • Light: Requires full sun for optimal growth and flavor.
  • Watering: Water every other day, allow the soil to dry out slightly between waterings.

Recipes

  • Classic Pesto: Blend with pine nuts, garlic, Parmesan, and olive oil to create a delicious pesto sauce for pasta or sandwiches.
  • Caprese Salad: Use fresh leaves in a Caprese salad with tomatoes and mozzarella.
  • Herb Butter: Mix chopped basil with softened butter for a savory spread on bread or vegetables.
