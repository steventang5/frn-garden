Mint

Benefits

  • Digestive Aid: Mint is well-known for soothing stomach discomfort and aiding digestion
  • Aromatherapy: Its pleasant aroma can help relieve stress and headaches.
  • Culinary Uses: It offers a mild mint flavor, making it a versatile addition to teas, salads, and desserts.

Maintenance

  • Light: Prefers partial shade(3-6 hours of sunlight) too much direct sunlight in hot climates might cause wilting.
  • Watering: Water daily when warm or every other day when cold to keep the soil moist but avoid waterlogging.

Recipes

  • Herbal Tea: Steep fresh leaves in hot water for a refreshing, calming tea.
  • Salad Garnish: Finely chop and sprinkle over salads for a burst of flavor.
  • Infused Water: Add a few leaves to a jug of water with lemon slices for a refreshing twist.
