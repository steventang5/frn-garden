Thyme

Benefits

  • Antimicrobial Properties: Thyme has natural antimicrobial and antiseptic qualities, which may help soothe coughs and colds.
  • Digestive Health: Known to support digestion and relieve minor gastrointestinal issues.
  • Culinary Versatility: Its subtle, earthy flavor makes it a great addition to a variety of dishes.

Maintenance

  • Light: Prefers partial shade(3-6 hours of sunlight) too much direct sunlight in hot climates might cause wilting.
  • Watering: Water daily during warm weather or every other day during cold weather adjusting based on soil dryness to avoid overwatering.

Recipes

  • Herb-Infused Oils: Steep fresh thyme in olive oil to create a flavorful infusion for dressings or cooking.
  • Roasted Dishes: Use as a seasoning in roasted meats and vegetables.
  • Soups and Stews: Add to broths and stews for a subtle herbal note that complements savory dishes.
